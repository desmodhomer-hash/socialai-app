Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Overview

SocialAI ("the App") is a desktop application developed by Luigi (Milano, Italy). This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your social media accounts.

2. Data We Collect

  • OAuth tokens: Access tokens for TikTok and Instagram, stored locally on your device in encrypted form.
  • Account information: Basic profile data (username, follower count) retrieved from connected platforms to display in the dashboard.
  • Content data: Video files and metadata you create within the App, stored locally on your device.
  • Analytics data: Post performance metrics fetched from TikTok and Instagram APIs, stored locally.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. The App does not operate a remote server or cloud database. OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet) before being stored in a local SQLite database.

4. Third-Party Platforms

The App connects to the following platforms via their official APIs:

  • TikTok: Used to publish videos and retrieve analytics. Governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Instagram / Meta: Used to publish videos and retrieve analytics. Governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only transmitted to TikTok and Instagram APIs as required to perform the functions you request (publishing posts, retrieving analytics).

6. Data Retention

Data is retained locally on your device for as long as you use the App. You can delete all data by uninstalling the App and removing its data directory.

7. Security

OAuth tokens are stored encrypted. API keys are stored in a local environment file. We recommend keeping your device secure and not sharing your App data directory.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your TikTok or Instagram account at any time through each platform's account settings. This will invalidate the stored tokens.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the developer via GitHub: github.com/desmodhomer-hash.
